SR-latch arbiter (vernier2d/srlatch) metastability characterization
Corner TT, 27 C, Vdd=1.8 V, 5 fF output loads, via srlatch_tb

Arbitration offset dt0          : +0.149 ps (S must lead by this to fire)
Dead zone (decision ambiguity)  : < 1 fs  (grid-limited bound)
Regeneration time constant tau  : 24.1 ps (fit over overdrive < 5 ps)
